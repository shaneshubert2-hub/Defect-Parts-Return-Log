Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Parts Return Log is a simple tool for logging defective auto parts and preparing them for manufacturer credit submissions.

Data collection

This app does not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server. There is no account, no sign-in, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — manufacturer names, part numbers, quantities, reasons, and photos — is stored only in your browser's local storage, on your own device. It is never sent anywhere. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, this information is deleted permanently.

Photos

Any photo you attach to a part is resized and stored locally alongside that entry. It is not uploaded, shared, or accessed by anyone but you.

Exports

The CSV export feature creates a file directly on your device for you to save or send yourself. This app has no part in transmitting that file anywhere.

Third parties

The app loads typefaces from Google Fonts when online. No other third-party service is used.

Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page.
